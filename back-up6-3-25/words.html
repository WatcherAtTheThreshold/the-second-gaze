Words & Reflections

"There is another world, but it is in this one."
— Paul Éluard

Sometimes paintings speak in silence. Other times, they need words to carry the tone forward. Here you’ll find Shannon’s thoughts, writings, musings on the creative process, and glimpses of the mythic threads woven through her work.

Whether it’s a short note from the studio, a poem, or an artist’s statement—these are the echoes behind the brush. This space will grow and shift, just as the paintings do.
